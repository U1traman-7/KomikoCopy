# Hey there, fellow creators!

Whether you're diving into the world of AI characters for the first time or you're a seasoned artist looking for a fresh spark, you've landed in the right place. I've roamed the digital universe and gathered a list of must-visit websites that are sure to ignite your creativity and help you bring your unique AI characters to life. So, grab your digital pens and let’s explore these gems!

## 1. **CharacterHub** - Your Creative Community

![Host your AI character on CharacterHub](/images/blog/20240820/1.png "Host your AI character on CharacterHub")

If CharacterHub isn't already your go-to, you’re definitely missing out! It’s the perfect spot to mingle with both novice and professional AI character designers. Dive into a sea of trending AI characters, snag design ideas, and get real-time feedback on your own sketches. The community is buzzing and incredibly supportive—ideal for collaborations or even landing a few commissions.

An AI character is incomplete without a [great backstory](/blog/20240825-How-to-Write-a-Captivating-Backstory-and-Lore-for-Your-Original-Character), so beware!

## 2. **KomikoAI App** - AI Character Creation Social App

![AI Character Creation on KomikoAI](/images/blog/20240820/2.png "Create AI Character on KomikoAI")

AI characters, anime-based AI comics, beautiful character creation, or community features — KomikoAI is an all-in-one solution for all your fictional needs. You can chat with AI characters, share photos with friends, or create AI comics much faster.

## 3. **BaM Animation** - Learn from Industry Giants

Curious how top TV studio pros craft those unforgettable AI characters? BaM Animation offers a peek behind the curtain. Led by industry veterans Brent Noll and Maximus Paulson, their videos are packed with neat tricks and tips, served up in bite-sized tutorials that are perfect for a creativity boost anytime you need it.

Check out [10 AI Chat Roleplay apps](/blog/20240821-10-Best-Character-AI-Apps-For-Chat-Lovers) if you want inspiration!

## 4. [**CharacterCreator.org**](http://charactercreator.org/) - Digital Dress-Up

![AI Character Creator](/images/blog/20240820/3.png "AI Character Creator")

This site takes AI character outfitting to a whole new level. At CharacterCreator.org, mix and match hairstyles, accessories, and costumes with just a few clicks. It’s an invaluable tool for those brainstorming sessions, and yes, it’s completely free! Perfect for crafting a diverse cast for your latest AI comic or AI chat storyline.

## 5. **Subjectively** - Pop Culture Meets Design

![Subjectively YouTube Channel](/images/blog/20240820/4.png "Subjectively YouTube Channel")

Ever seen a Pokémon get a design makeover? Check out Subjectively on YouTube, where a group of friends reimagine popular AI characters. It’s relaxed, fun, and incredibly inspiring, especially if you're looking to see how current trends can influence AI character design.

## 6. **Character Design References** - An Artist’s Treasure Trove

This website is like striking gold in the world of AI character design. Character Design References offers a vast array of visual inspiration, from detailed tutorials to interviews with seasoned pros. It’s the go-to resource for those deep-dive days when you’re committed to upping your AI character design game.

## 7. [**CharacterDesigns.com**](http://characterdesigns.com/) - For the Perfect Pose

Need to nail that dynamic pose? Hong Ly’s CharacterDesigns.com offers an extensive collection of high-quality images for free, ranging from dynamic action poses to everyday scenarios. It’s a favorite among fantasy artists looking for that perfect magical or combat stance for their AI characters in AI comics or AI chat stories.

Here is an in-depth resource on [AI character design](/blog/20240823-how-to-character-design-original-character) for the brave.

## 8. **Picrew** - Explore Style Variations

Imagine your AI character in countless different styles without having to redraw them each time. Picrew lets you experiment with user-created AI character generators, providing fresh perspectives on your AI characters that can fuel even more creativity.

## 9. **ArtStation** - The Professional’s Platform

ArtStation is where the pros play. This platform showcases breathtaking AI character art portfolios and is a fountain of inspiration whether you’re looking to learn, admire, or even scout out potential hires for collaborative AI chat or AI comic projects.

## 10. **DeviantArt** - The Classic Art Network

![Deviant Art Landing Page](/images/blog/20240820/8.png "Deviant Art Landing Page")

DeviantArt remains a vital hub for AI character artists, offering a diverse range of styles from anime to photorealism. It’s a vibrant community where artists of all levels share, explore, and discuss their work, especially in the realm of AI chat and AI comics.

## 11. **Winged Canvas** - Virtual Art School

Looking to sharpen your AI character design skills? Winged Canvas offers numerous free video tutorials that cover the gamut from basic drawing techniques to complex AI character design. Their approachable, easy-to-follow lessons are great for both beginners and advanced artists focused on AI chat or AI comics.

## Closing Thoughts

With these sites bookmarked, you’re all set to take your AI character design skills to new heights. Each platform offers unique tools, insights, and communities that are invaluable to both budding and veteran AI character creators. So, start exploring, keep sketching, and don’t forget to share your AI characters and discoveries with the broader community. Let’s keep inspiring and creating together, whether in AI chat, AI comics, or beyond. Happy designing!
