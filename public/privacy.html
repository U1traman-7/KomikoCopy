This privacy notice for KomikoAI ("we," "us," or "our"), operated by Caffelabs Inc., describes how and why we might collect, store, use, and/or share ("process") your information when you use our services ("Services"), such as when you:

Personal Data We Collect

We collect information that you voluntarily provide to us when you register on the Services, express an interest in obtaining information about us or our products and Services, participate in activities on the Services, or otherwise contact us.

The personal information we collect may include the following:

  • Email addresses
  • Usernames
  • Passwords

We do not knowingly collect or process sensitive categories of personal data (such as health, biometric, or financial account numbers) unless strictly necessary and with your explicit consent.

Personal Data We Obtain From Third Parties

Single Sign-On (SSO): If you sign up to our website KomikoAI through third-party services such as Google, you authorize us to collect authentication information, such as your username and encrypted access credentials, and other personal data available through the third-party application account, including the following:

  • Google: You can sign up and log in to KomikoAI using your Google account. When you sign up using Google, Google will request your permission to share certain information from your Google account with KomikoAI. This includes your Google email address and avatar (optional). This information is collected by Google and provided to us under the terms of Google’s privacy policy. You can control the information we receive from Google using your Google activity controls. If you signed up using Google by mistake, you can delete your account from the Account page within your Settings at any time, or you can contact us by emailing contact@komiko.app.

How We Use Your Personal Data

Our legal bases for processing your personal data include your consent, the performance of a contract (providing our Services), compliance with legal obligations, and our legitimate interests in operating, improving, and securing the Services.

We use your personal data to:

  • Provide, Improve, and Develop the Platform: We process your personal data primarily to ensure the adequate performance of our website and Services. We may also process your personal data to pursue our legitimate interest in improving the KomikoAI platform and providing our users with the best experience. This includes
    • Operating and maintaining your KomikoAI account, including managing your zaps balance, and providing access to and use of the platform.
    • Identifying you as the creator of content you upload, comments you post, and/or other contributions you make to the website.
    • Responding to any comments or inquiries you submit.
  • Maintain a Safe and Secure Environment: We process your personal data to enhance the security of the platform (including the security of your personal data) to provide our users with the best experience.

How We Share Your Personal Data

We do not sell your personal data to external parties.

  • Through Your Profile: Any personal data you choose to disclose in your public profile on KomikoAI will be accessible to other users of the website, who may view your profile information, view, and comment on any of your public content.
  • Third-Party Applications: If you choose to connect your KomikoAI account to other apps or social networks, such as Google, Discord, or Twitter, you may share certain personal data with these apps or networks. Content shared with social media networks might appear on your timeline (or similar functions) and in feeds that may be viewed by the public or specific followers, subject to the terms and conditions of those social media networks.
  • Service Providers: We may share your personal data with trusted third-party service providers who perform services on our behalf. These include providers of cloud hosting (e.g., Vercel, AWS), data storage (e.g., Supabase, AWS), AI model services (e.g., OpenAI, Google Gemini), analytics services (e.g., Google Analytics), customer support tools, and payment processors (e.g., Stripe).
  • Compliance with Laws: We may disclose your personal data to courts, law enforcement agencies, or governmental authorities to:
    • Comply with our legal obligations.
    • Respond to legal processes or claims asserted against KomikoAI.
    • Address verified requests related to criminal investigations or alleged illegal activities that may expose us, you, or other users to legal liability.
    • Enforce and administer our Terms of Use and other agreements with users.
    • Protect the rights, property, or personal safety of KomikoAI, its employees, and members of the public.

Advertising and Monetization

KomikoAI may display advertising or sponsored content within the Services. In connection with these activities, we may process or allow our advertising partners to process limited information to serve, deliver, and measure ads.

  • Data Used for Advertising: This may include device and browser information, approximate location (e.g., country or city), session metadata, content context, engagement signals (such as ad impressions or clicks), and pseudonymized identifiers (such as hashed IDs or cookies).
  • Types of Advertising:
    • Contextual Ads: Based on the content you are currently viewing, with no cross-site tracking.
    • Personalized (Targeted) Ads: Where you have given consent or where permitted by law, we may use signals to display ads relevant to your interests.
  • Sharing vs. Selling: While we do not sell your personal data for monetary value, the disclosure of certain technical identifiers to advertising partners for personalized advertising may be defined as "sharing" or "targeted advertising" under certain US privacy laws (California, Colorado, Connecticut, etc.).
  • Your Controls: Users have the right to opt out of personalized advertising via our "Cookie Preferences" manager, Global Privacy Control (GPC) signals, or device settings. We do not knowingly serve interest-based advertising to individuals under 18.
